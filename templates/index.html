Artisanal Weaving Heritage

Our bathik collection represents centuries-old Sri Lankan weaving traditions. Each piece is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans using natural fibers and traditional techniques.

From intricate table runners to elegant shawls, every item tells a story of cultural heritage and sustainable craftsmanship.

Our Sri Lankan Heritage

Founded in 2010, Lanka Bazaar began as a small initiative to support rural artisans in Sri Lanka. Today, we work with over 200 craftsmen across the island, preserving traditional techniques while providing sustainable livelihoods.

Each product tells a story - from the intricate hand-weaving of sarees to the careful crafting of handloom fabrics. We're committed to authenticity, quality, and ethical practices in everything we do.
